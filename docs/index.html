  • Categories

    There are three categories of books currently:

    Exploring Spiritual Reality. These books explore directly themes related to religion, the life of the soul, and prayer. These books draw on the Teachings of Bahá'u'lláh. To learn more about the Bahá'í Faith.
    Quality Stories. These stories are fictional and explore a particular spiritual quality, such as obedience, humility or reverence.
    Exploring Material Reality. These books explore the physical world, and all its animals, plants,minerals and systems.

  • Navigation

    The shop is in the top right corner.

    Once you have selected a book it will appear in your box.
    Tap the book in your box to read it.
    Swipe left or right to navigate pages, and tap on the page to toggle reader mode.

  • Purpose

    The bookbox is a way to share the books I make for my own children with others, at as little cost as possible.
